Restaurant Story (About Us):

Founded by Chef Jessica Bassaw in 2024, Ethereal Bites is a tribute to the diversity of Africa's culinary heritage.
Chef Jessica, raised in Ghana, has worked with top chefs across the continent.
Her vision was to bring traditional African flavors to a global audience in a refined, sustainable manner.
Ethereal Bites creates an authentic yet modern dining experience that tells the story of Africa through food.
